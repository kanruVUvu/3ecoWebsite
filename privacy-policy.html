We collect Information of two kinds: (a) Personal Information and; (b) Non-personal Information. The various ways in which Information may be collected is detailed hereinbelow.

(a) Information provided by You voluntarily:

We may collect Information through various means and in various places through the Platform, including Account Registration forms, Contact Us forms, or when You otherwise interact with us. We shall ask You to provide only such Information which is for lawful purpose connected with Our Services and necessary to be collected by us for such purpose.

The current data fields that might be requested for are:

  • OTP
  • Name

  • Mobile Phone Number

We may collect from You, “Personal Information”, which is Information collected that can be used to uniquely identify or contact You. Apart from the kind of Information as listed above, We may also collect from You Information regarding Your transactions on the Website, Internet Protocol address, any other items of “sensitive personal data or Information” as such term is defined under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data Of Information) Rules, 2011 enacted under the Information Technology Act, 2000, identification code of Your communication device which You use to access the Website or any other Information that You provide during Your registration process, if any, on the Website. We may also enable features which requires Our access to Your address book, calendar, photo gallery etc.

Additionally, when You visit the Platform, We may collect certain “Non-personal Information” such as, operating system, browser type, , aggregate user data pages You request, number of clicks, search history and result selected, active seconds or minutes on the Platform, frequency of Your visit to the Platform, the date and time of Your visit to Platform or use of the Services and cookie information, etc. This type of Information however, does not identify You personally.

(b) Automatic Information:

In addition to Information which You provide to Us voluntarily, We also collect Information from You and related to You during the course of Your usage of Our Services on the Platform. This Usage Information may include the browser that You are using, the URL that referred You to Our Services, all of the areas within Our Services that You visit, and the time of day, among other Information. In addition, We collect Information about Your Device.

Additionally, the same procedure of collection of call records may take place when calls are placed for redressal of customer grievances by way of contacting Our customer care.

Usage Information may be collected using “Cookies”. If You do not want Information to be collected through the use of cookies, Your browser allows You to deny or accept the use of cookies.
